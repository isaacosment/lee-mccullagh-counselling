Renew & Revitalise

Toowoomba Relationship & Individual Counselling

I am an experienced, professionally trained counsellor offering counselling for couples and individuals in Toowoomba.

I can help you to discuss, understand and work through issues you are facing, in a comfortable and professional environment.

Relationship Counselling

I can help you to identify, discuss and address underlying issues that may be negatively impacting on your relationship.

Improve, renew & revitalise the quality of your relationship through improved communication and knowledge of each other.

Individual Counselling

I can support you in learning and understanding more about yourself. Improve, renew and revitalise self esteem, cope with life stressors, manage anxiety, health / cancer issues and bereavement.

I use a client-centred approach providing empathy, unconditional positive regard and congruence.

About Lee McCullagh

When working with you I value kindness, honesty & respect.

I am professionally trained in counselling and nursing to help you be seen and heard, to find clarity, overcome barriers, make change, plan and grow more resilient as an individual or couple.

Qualifications & Experience

  • Relationship counsellor 3 years with Relationships Australis
  • Australian Counselling Association (ACA) Level 2 Member
  • Diploma in counselling (Australian Institute of Professional Counsellors)
  • Certificate I Gottman Method couples Therapy (Gottman Institute)
  • Diploma in Grief & Bereavement counselling (Centre of Excellence, UK)
  • NCFE level 2 Certificate in counselling skills (England, Wales & North Ireland)

Additional Credentials

  • Registered nurse (30 years) - Working in surgical & medical wards, theatres and discharge suites in England, Canada and Australia.
  • Nurse educator in England and Australia.
